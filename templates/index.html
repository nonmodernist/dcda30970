Special Topics: Digital Infrastructure & Climate Crisis

Digital Infrastructure and Climate Crisis

Exploring the material reality behind digital abstractions

"The internet is inextricably tied to the coming horrors of the climate crisis. It is both a major force behind that crisis and one of its likely casualties."

â€”Kevin Lozano, "Can the Internet Survive Climate Change?"
Course Edition
Fall 2025
Dr. Alexandra Edwards

We live in an era of contradictions: on the one hand, the infrastructures that make digital culture possible now criss-cross our planet and even surround it from space, and the tech industry's massive gamble on so-called "artificial intelligence" suggests that the physical resources dedicated to digital technologies will only increase in the near future. On the other hand, climate crisis is here, fueled by the energy-related emissions that digital infrastructure significantly contributes to, threatening the very material foundations of a digital society.

Course Philosophy

This course operates at the intersection of theory and practice, challenging students to think critically about digital infrastructure while developing practical skills to address the environmental impacts of technology.
